Hackdays

Technology is changing the world, and our expectations. Corporate IT is lagging behind personal IT

The NHS can't afford to lag behind

The world is becoming more digital and so is health
The medium used to support, and deliver health, matters
The medium should be digital but we must do it right

We believe that innovation happens when you give people freedom.
Which is why we run Hack Days.

Hack Days are all about intensive collaboration. They allow you to sidestep the bureaucracy that slows down innovation, and rapidly realise fresh ideas. When you bring together multidisciplinary domain experts and inspire them, amazing things happen.

Most Hack Days last just a couple days, but what you get from that time is extraordinary. The intense focus towards a common goal turns ideas into reality before more traditional methods would be done deciding whether it was a good idea.
